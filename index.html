Regions

Aragoria

The country of Aragoria has existed for many centuries, with the first inhabitants being the original Mons people, whose descendants remain there to this day. Little else is known about the country as a whole, only that it is surrounded by water with no visible land in sight from any direction.

Cities

Portum

The country of Aragoria has existed for many centuries, with the first inhabitants being the original Mons people, whose descendants remain there to this day. Little else is known about the country as a whole, only that it is surrounded by water with no visible land in sight from any direction.

Villages

Molto

Molto is a small village on the southern point of Aragoria with a short history, having been formed alongside neighbouring villages a few hundred years ago, the inhabitants migrated from a distant land, over the sea.

Oblitus

Deriving it’s name from the forgotten ancients that previously inhabited the forest, Oblitus covers the majority of Aragoria, and some have claimed to have seen relics of the past located within its confines, only to find nothing upon returning. Many a villager has gone mad trying to rediscover what they once saw.

Emit

Emit is a village created on the embers of a crumbling city, destroyed by a meteor in ages past. Although the village has maintained a stable existence, being a shell of its former self, the residents cannot seem to move on.

Mons

Mons is a village that used to be a beautiful paradise but ever since the Imperium arrived 150 years ago the state of the village has declined as if the land rejected their presence. Despite this the Mons villagers have tried to maintain the tranquility and peace of previous generations, seeing themselves rarely get involved with anything not involving their village.

Characters

William

The main character William is a 16 year old boy who was born and raised in the village of Molto. His father left so long ago that he can’t remember him at all, so he was raised by his mother. He possesses a rare aptitude for magic, being that of fire and has an inquisitive nature, with a streak of sarcasm.

Vanessa

Vanessa is a 16 year old girl who was born and spent the majority of her life with her parents in the village of Emit, until a few months prior to the story's beginning, when a group of bandits, controlled by the Imperium, were sent to eradicate those that fought and capture those that wouldn’t, her parents were killed in the process. This drives her to seek vengeance on the Imperium for indirectly murdering her parents. Despite this she still has a relatively positive personality, with a hint of proudness, which she got from her parents. At the beginning of the story she will do whatever she can to get back at the Imperium, but as time progresses she realises she can’t do it alone. She also possesses a rare aptitude for magic, being that of ice.

Lauren

Lauren is William's mother and has raised him by herself from an early age. She is seen as a gentle, understanding, mother figure. Strangely enough, even though William possesses magic, she is never seen using it and can be assumed to not have the ability to use it. She is a docile person, but will do anything to protect her son.

Imperium Leader

The Imperium leader is an older figure that travelled across distant lands to extend his reach to Aragoria, and uses the bandits in the area, along with a bevy of his own soldiers to control the people. The land from where he originated is shrouded in mystery and whether he is the mastermind behind this remains to be seen.

Mons Elder

The Mons village Elder is a direct descendant of the original Mons and passes her wisdom through her words, as she heard it as a child. She remembers how the stories went, and how the village used to be, and holds regret for what it’s become. Many a fellow Mons villager has praised her for what she has done, but she can’t let go of seeing the village as it once was.

Factions

Bandits

The bandits are those who prefer violence to negotiation. They are known to drift from place to place, taking what they wish and terrorising villages. They were rather directionless before the Imperium showed up and gave them direction. They are seen as a roaming bunch and will only stay in one place if ordered to do so. They see gold as the highest commodity, and you know what they say, there’s no honour amongst thieves.

The Imperium

The Imperium are a group of elitists, believing in the need for one leader to control the people. They are based in Portum and use their influence to scare the cities residents into staying. The Imperium also extends to the bandits, as they hired them to clear out the villages and send the people who surrendered to Portum making it a city of people that would rather be anywhere else. The majority of the people in the Imperium came with the leader, but there are a few people who agreed with their way of living and joined them willingly.

Collectables

Collectables are obtainable ingame through completing tasks, experience is one of them to help level your character, there are some cosmetics, such as hats that can be found throughout the game by completing puzzles and discovering areas. Another collectable found in certain areas are crystals, that can be used to upgrade certain parts of abilities, for example, the duration of the firewall, or the damage of the fireball.

Creatures

Toxicus Boletus

The mushrooms that can be seen wandering the Oblitus forest are a result of the access/excess of magical energy that the Tree’s of Life give off. Each mutated to be dangerous to be in it’s vacinity.

Canem Mortum

The Canem Mortum are a variant of dog, changed by the magical energies in the area they live. They can be seen wandering the caverns and forest, devoid of purpose, driven mad by the Imperium’s controlling ways. Many have been turned savage as a result of witnessing the bandits dirty work, for self preservation.

Egestas

The bunnies are docile creatures, untainted by the Imperiums evil. They can be seen wandering the Oblitus forest, harmless to anyone.
